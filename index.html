We offer aluminum based gates & fences. All our fences & gates are made of few aluminum components which you see below , it is very easy to assemble them and put them together.anyone with basic DIY skills and tools mentioned below can assemble these.
These components make different styles of fence, gate & railings, there are also videos on our website that shows how to assemble these gates & fences.

Components:

Main Post:
For gate frames and fence & rail posts. 80 cm * 80 cm ( length: any size up to 3.6 meter) Each post have 3 grooves , usually one side of the post will have the cover and in other two grooves fence slats and spacers ( to create space between fence panels)will seat. ( please look at videoes) . £38 per meter

Post cover:
To cover grooves that are not being used in the post, each post comes with two covers, for fence post & railing you usually use two groves , so you will have one extra cover that can be cut to any size, and use it as spacer each post comes with two free post cover

25 mm aluminium Slat:
to be used as smaller fence, gate & rail slat, 25 mm in any length up to 3.6 meter. £9 per meter

L Brackets
with Screws to secure the aluminium fence or gate panels to post or frame , in each fence or gate panel you need to secure only handful of slats. £8 each

Post Cap:
Aluminum post cap To be used as a top cap for the post. price-= £6 each

Flat post cap:
Aluminum post cap. To be used as a top cap for the post. price-= £4 each

Post base :
to secure fence panel to flat surface ( concrete, wood, etc), £7 each

steel tube:
1.8 meter to secure fence panel inside soil or concrete. £12 each

130 mm aluminium slat:
to be used as larger fence, gate & rail slat, 130b mm in any length up to 3.6 meter. £15 per meter

Aluminium spacers:
to create space between slats( each fence have spare cover that can be used to create your own custom size spacers) £5 for 20 * 1 cm spacer

Tools needed:

Here are the main tools you need to assemble these ( mitre saw with alluminium blade, Impact driver, drill driver, measure tape, spirit level, rafter squre), if you are attaching the posts to wall, etc, you will need metal bits to make a hole in post. ( see videos)

How to place an order:

1. Look at the projects on our website, decide which style you like , you can also have your own design, all our fences & gates have been made with one smaller ( 25 mm ) and one larger ( 130 mm) slat and you can create your own by using any size of spacer and any combination between them.
2. Take measurements and decide how many fencel panels & gate do you need.
3. Look at our components and decide which ones and how many you need according to your design ( you can also email us if you need help)
4. Please contact us via email and tell us what components and what quantity you need and we will send you an invoice.
5. If you need sample, we can send you sample for a fee.
